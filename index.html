About Me

Hi, I'm Brett. I live in Los Angeles. I grew up in rural New Jersey. I was a currency, commodity and bond trader in New York for almost 20 years. Since 2013, I have been working as an expert witness in litigation relating to derivatives trading. I spent 2018-19 auditing courses here at UCLA in the linguistics department. I thought I wanted to get a Ph.D. in linguistics. But 5-7 years is too long at my advanced age. So now I am here at bootcamp, learning my next career--web development! I live in central L.A. (Hancock Park neighborhood) with my wife and 3 kids and, thankfully, zero pets. I became a nerd in around 2001, about the time I started using linux and the Dvorak keyboard layout.
